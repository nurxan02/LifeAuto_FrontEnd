TO START WORKING, ALL YOU NEED TO DO IS:

  • Step 1:

    You Are 18 or Older To legally operate as a driver,
    you must be of legal age.

  • Step 2:

    You Hold a Polish Category B Driver’s License A valid
    Polish driver's license is required to ensure
    compliance with local regulations.

  • Step 3:

    Criminal Record Certificate This certificate is
    necessary to obtain a passenger transport license, in
    accordance with legal standards.

  • Step 4:

    You Own a Smartphone A smartphone with internet access
    is a vital tool for receiving and managing ride
    requests.

  • Step 5:

    Your Car Is No Older Than 2004 For safety reasons,
    your car must meet certain age requirements. If you
    don’t have a suitable vehicle, no worries—we offer
    rental options so you can focus on your work.
